Portfolio Highlights

Here are some pieces from my portfolio that I am either very proud or were otherwise an important part of my education / career.

Optimised Raytracer

In our third year we were tasked with optimising a raytracing algorithm using low level programming techniques. We were also tasked with porting the program to linux and, while I started it and got marks for that, I did not finish that section. Regardless, my outlined method was sound enough to gain marks and I am very proud of how the project turned out. Portfolio

Semestor 2 DirectX work

During my second year at university I was tasked building on my previous DirectX object. This involved adding more complex things to the program such as textures, physics, lighting etc. This is one of the most impactful projects from my time at university as I learned not only how to do these things in DirectX, but how they are done in general.
