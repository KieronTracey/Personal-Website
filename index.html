Hi, I'm Kieron!

I am currently a student of Staffordshire University studying Games Programming. For as long as I can remember it has been a dream of mine to work in the games industry creating experiences similar to those that defined my childhood. It is my hope that studying here will set me on the path to aiding in the creation of similar experiences for the next generation.
